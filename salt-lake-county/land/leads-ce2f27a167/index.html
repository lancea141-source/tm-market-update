Approach

"I track land pricing in Salt Lake City and the surrounding cities — I run a regression on every closed lot sale in the county. Your parcel came up in the analysis. The number I get is different from where it was listed, and I think the gap explains why it sat. Can I walk you through what the sales actually show? No obligation — worst case you get a clearer picture than you had."
For the ones that never cut: avoid implying they were greedy. The honest framing is that they never received the market's answer. For the ones that cut and still failed: lead with that — they already tried the obvious lever, which proves price alone was not the whole problem and opens the door to exposure, positioning and buyer targeting.
